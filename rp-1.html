RP-1

RP-1 (acronym for Refined Petroleum 1 - Refined Kerosene 1) is a fuel used in the propellant engines of space rockets. RP-1 is derived from petroleum and is similar to kerosene, it has a very characteristic smell. It is usually oxidized by liquid oxygen (LOX), with which it burns at 3,670 K (3,396.85 °C or 6,146.33 °F). Rockets such as Atlas, Delta, Molniya, Titan I, Saturn IB, Saturn V, Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy used the RP-1 as fuel in the first or all of their stages.
